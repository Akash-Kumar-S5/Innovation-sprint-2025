GreenGrow Innovations is a sustainable agriculture company founded in 2012. It is headquartered in Austin, Texas, and focuses on creating organic, eco-friendly fertilizers and soil enhancement solutions.

The company offers a wide range of products including:
- BioBoost Organic Fertilizer
- AquaRoot Water Retention Gel
- EcoGrow Soil Enrichers

GreenGrow serves clients across North America and partners with local farms to promote sustainable farming practices. Their mission is to make agriculture more efficient, environmentally friendly, and profitable for farmers.

In 2023, GreenGrow launched its Research and Development Center to expand into climate-resilient crops and AI-powered precision farming tools.
